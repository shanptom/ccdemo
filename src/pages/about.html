About Casual Coffee Shop

Your neighborhood coffee spot since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by local coffee enthusiasts, Casual Coffee Shop has been serving the Coffeeville community with premium coffee and delicious pastries. What started as a small kiosk has grown into a beloved local gathering spot.

Our mission is simple: provide high-quality coffee and food in a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate baristas and food enthusiasts who are dedicated to providing the best experience for our customers. Each team member undergoes extensive training to ensure they can craft the perfect cup of coffee and provide excellent service.

We believe in giving back to the community that supports us. Casual Coffee Shop regularly participates in local events, sponsors youth sports teams, and donates to local charities. We also host monthly open mic nights to showcase local talent.
